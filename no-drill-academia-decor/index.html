First, the renter's rules

How to decorate damage-free

Five no-drill moves that keep every wall intact.

  1. 1Peel, don't paint. Removable wallpaper on one wall does the heavy lifting — and lifts off clean.
  2. 2Stick the lights. Battery or adhesive sconces give candlelit walls with zero wiring.
  3. 3Tension, don't screw. A tension rod hangs curtains and closes off a nook without a fixing.
  4. 4Hang on strips. Damage-free strips and hooks hold frames and art — save the packaging to remove them tidily.
  5. 5Lean and stack. Leaning shelves and frames need nothing but a wall to rest on.
